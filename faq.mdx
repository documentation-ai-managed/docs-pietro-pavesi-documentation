## Account and Billing

Tacivo handles all your subscription needs securely. Review these common queries to manage your account effectively.

<ExpandableGroup>

<Expandable title="How do I update my billing information?">

Navigate to your account settings dashboard. Click on the `Billing` tab, then select `Update Payment Method`. Enter your new card details and save changes. Tacivo uses Stripe for secure processing, so expect confirmation within seconds.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What payment methods do you accept?">

Tacivo accepts major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express via Stripe. Invoices for enterprise plans are available upon request.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Contact sales@Tacivo.com for wire transfer options on annual enterprise subscriptions.
</Callout>

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I cancel my subscription anytime?">

Yes, cancel from the `Billing` section in account settings. Subscriptions end at the current billing cycleâ€”no prorated refunds, but you retain access until the period expires.

</Expandable>

</ExpandableGroup>

Maximize Tacivo's AI knowledge capture by following these guidelines during conversations.

<Tabs>

## Data Privacy and Security

Tacivo prioritizes your data protection.

<Expandable title="Is my conversation data encrypted?">

Yes, all data transmits over HTTPS/TLS 1.3 and stores encrypted at rest using AES-256. You control data retention and can delete conversations anytime.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Who can access my Tacivo data?">

Only you and authorized team members on shared workspaces. Tacivo employees never access content without explicit consent for support.

<Callout kind="success">
  Comply with GDPR, SOC 2 Type II, and HIPAA upon request.
</Callout>

</Expandable>

    Refresh your browser or try incognito mode.

  </Step>

</Steps>
