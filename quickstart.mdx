## Overview

Tacivo helps you capture expert knowledge through AI-powered conversations, turning tacit expertise into structured, searchable documentation. Follow these steps to create your account, set up a workspace, start your first session, and navigate the interface. You complete setup in under 5 minutes and begin building your knowledge base immediately.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use a dedicated email for your Tacivo account to keep notifications organized.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up quickly using your preferred method.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email" icon="mail">
    Visit [app.tacivo.com/signup](https://app.tacivo.com/signup) and enter your email address and password.
    
    Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Google" icon="apple">
    Click "Sign up with Google" on the signup page.
    
    Authorize Tacivo to access your Google account for seamless login.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Configure Your Workspace

After account creation, set up your first workspace to organize conversations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Workspace" icon="folder">
    From the dashboard, click **New Workspace**.
    
    Enter a name like "Engineering Knowledge Base" and select your team size.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Team Members" icon="users">
    Invite collaborators by email.
    
    Set roles: Admin for full access, Viewer for read-only.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize Settings" icon="settings">
    Enable features like real-time collaboration and auto-export to Markdown.
    
    Paste your `{API_KEY}` if integrating with external tools.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<CodeGroup tabs="JSON,YAML">
```json
{
  "workspaceId": "ws_1234567890",
  "features": {
    "realTime": true,
    "autoExport": "markdown"
  },
  "apiKey": "your-api-key-here"
}
```

```yaml
workspaceId: ws_1234567890
features:
  realTime: true
  autoExport: markdown
apiKey: your-api-key-here
```
</CodeGroup>

## Initiate Your First Conversation

Start capturing knowledge right away.

1. Select your workspace from the sidebar.
2. Click **New Conversation** and choose a topic like "Onboarding Process".
3. Chat with the AI: Ask "Explain our deployment workflow" and refine responses.
4. Tacivo structures your input into searchable docs automatically.
